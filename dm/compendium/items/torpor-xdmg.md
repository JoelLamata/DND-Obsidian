# Torpor
*Adventuring gear, poison (ingested)*  


- **Properties**: [Poison](/3-Mechanics/CLI/item-properties.md#Poison)
- **Cost**: 600 gp

A creature subjected to Torpor poison must succeed on a DC 15 Constitution saving throw or have the [Poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) condition for `dice:4d6|noform|noparens|avg` (`4d6`) hours. The creature's [Speed](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/speed-xphb.md) is halved while the creature is [Poisoned](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Poisoned) in this way.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 91. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span>*
