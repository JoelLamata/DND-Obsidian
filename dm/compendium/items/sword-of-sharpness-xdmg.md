# Sword of Sharpness
*Very rare (requires attunement)*  
![](items/XDMG/Sword of Sharpness.webp#right)


When you attack an object with this magic weapon and hit, maximize your weapon damage dice against the target.

When you attack a creature with this weapon and roll a 20 on the `dice:d20|noform|noparens|avg` (`d20`) for the attack roll, that target takes an extra 14 Slashing damage and gains 1 [Exhaustion](/3-Mechanics/CLI/conditions.md#Exhaustion) level.

- [Longsword of Sharpness](#Longsword%20of%20Sharpness)
- [Scimitar of Sharpness](#Scimitar%20of%20Sharpness)
