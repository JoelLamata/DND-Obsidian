# Ring of Cold Resistance
*Ring, rare*  



You have [Resistance](/3-Mechanics/CLI/variant-rules/resistance-xphb.md) to cold damage while wearing this ring. The ring is set with tourmaline.

*Source: Dungeon Master's Guide (2024) p. 294. Available in the <span title='Systems Reference Document (5.2)'>SRD</span> and the Free Rules (2024)*
